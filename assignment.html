Empowa: Empowering Women to maximize their potentials

- Lasting Communities

Empowa is a Nigerian non-governmental-organization funded by and in partnership with well-meaning Nierians and International bodies. It seeks to increase the number of women who participate in and lead in business and political organizations in the 36 states in Nigeria. The project in Thailand strives to increase women's leadership, participation, and access to business opportunities within the private sector to advance sustainable and inclusive growth.This is accomplished through awareness-raising activities and engaging government agencies and private sector companies in policy discussions and dialogues to advance women's economic empowerment.
